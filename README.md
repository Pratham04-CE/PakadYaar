# PakadYaar
"PakadYaar" is a real-time multiplayer social deduction game where players join rooms, receive secret roles, and work together to identify the hidden impostor. Featuring multilingual support, customizable categories, live voting, chat, and AI-powered gameplay enhancements for an engaging experience.

# 🎭 PakadYaar

> **Can you catch the imposter before it's too late?**

PakadYaar is a multiplayer social deduction party game where players must identify the hidden imposter through discussion, observation, and voting. Every player receives a secret word. Most players receive the same word, while the imposter receives a different but related word. The twist? **The imposter doesn't know they are the imposter.**

Players take turns describing their assigned word without revealing it directly. After the discussion ends, everyone votes for who they believe is the imposter.

Will the players expose the imposter, or will the imposter deceive everyone and claim victory?

# 🕹️ Gameplay

## Step 1

Choose a game mode.

- Online
- Offline

---

## Step 2

Configure the game.

The Game Host selects:

- Number of Rounds
- Number of Imposters (1–3)
- Word Category
- Discussion Time
- Voting Time

---

## Step 3

Secret Word Assignment

Each player receives a secret word.

Example

Players:

```
Pizza
```

Imposter:

```
Burger
```

The imposter is **NOT informed** that they are the imposter.

Everyone simply sees their assigned word.

---

## Step 4

Discussion Phase

Each player describes their word without saying the word itself.

Example

Player A

> "It's something people usually eat with friends."

Player B

> "You can order it online."

Player C

> "Kids love it."

The goal is to identify inconsistencies while avoiding revealing the actual word.

---

## Step 5

Voting Phase

Once the timer ends, every player votes for who they believe is the imposter.

Each player gets one vote.

---

## Step 6

Results

### ✅ Players Win

If the real imposter receives the highest number of votes.

---

### 😈 Imposter Wins

If players vote for the wrong person.

---

### 🤝 Draw

If voting results in a tie:

- Discussion time is increased by **2 minutes**
- Players discuss again
- Another voting round begins

---

# 📜 Rules

- Never reveal your secret word directly.
- Describe your word carefully.
- Listen closely to everyone.
- Vote before the timer ends.
- Every player gets one vote.
- The imposter does not know they are the imposter.
- If voting ends in a draw, discussion continues for an additional 2 minutes.
- The game ends immediately once the voting result is finalized.
